Flame weeding is a simple, cost efficient, non-toxic weed control for hard to weed crops and orchards. Saves hours of hand weeding. Ideal for pre-emergence weed control on slow germinating crops such as carrots, beets, onions,  parsnips, etc.

These light-weight flamers were developed and tested in our own market garden. We are pleased to make them available to our fellow farmers and market gardeners.

The 4 and 5-torch walk-behind models fill the niche between a single hand-held torch and the tractor-mounted tool bar models. They are ideal for market gardens and CSAs.

  A High volume pressure regulator prevents problems with freeze-up and keeps an even flame pattern. It is also an essential safety feature as it reduces pressure on hoses and guards against massive flame. We consider this a non-optional accessory. But since some people already have a high volume regulator (1-2 million BTU), we sell it as an option.
